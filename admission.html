Admissions Open

Straightforward steps from enquiry to enrolment

Joining St Xavier School is simple and parent-friendly. Our process is transparent, guided, and designed to support you from enquiry to confirmed admission.

Age Criteria

Eligibility is based on the child’s age as on 31 March of the academic year. Typical age ranges for each level are:

Nursery 3 – 4 years
LKG 4 – 5 years
UKG 5 – 6 years
Class I 6 – 7 years

The office will confirm final eligibility after verifying your documents.

Please Note

  • Seats are allotted subject to availability in the applied class.
  • The school may conduct an interaction or assessment as per its policy.
  • All information and documents submitted must be accurate and valid.
  • Details on transport, uniform, etc. will be provided upon confirmation of admission.

Admission Process

  1. Enquiry and campus visit Call or drop in at the office to get forms, brochures, and have your questions answered.
  2. Application Complete the form with correct details of the child and parent/guardian.
  3. Documents Submit the filled form with attested copies of all required documents.
  4. Interaction / assessment If required, the child will be called for a short interaction or level-appropriate assessment.
  5. Admission confirmation After review, selected students receive confirmation and complete fee formalities.

For fee details and current deadlines, please get in touch with the school office.

Key school norms

  • Attendance must be regular and punctual.
  • Neat uniform is compulsory on all school days and official events.
  • Courtesy and respect towards staff and peers are non-negotiable.
  • Mobiles and personal gadgets are not allowed in school hours.
  • We expect parents to attend PTMs and stay in touch with the school.

Indicative timeline

  • Forms available: Early January
  • Last date for submission: End of March
  • Interactions/assessments: February–April
  • Offers and confirmation: As announced by the school

Required documents

  • Attested copy of birth certificate
  • Latest report card (if transferring)
  • Transfer certificate (where applicable)
  • Passport-size photographs of the child
  • Parent/guardian ID and address proof
  • Medical or allergy details, if any
